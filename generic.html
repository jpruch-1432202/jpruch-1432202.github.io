Who is Jake?

An experienced young professional who's ready to help you

Jake started his career at Mercer Island High School near Seattle, WA. Most notably, he was a wrestling team captain and was active in the community, running the city's Relay for Life chapter for 2 years. He also worked as a tutor, helping him develop critical teaching skills.

At UW, Jake began to thrive. He joined the Alpha Epsilon Pi Fraternity as a freshman, and by the end of that year he was voted to be the treasurer for his sophomore year. During that sophomore year he worked as the treasurer of the fraternity and worked part time at Eureka!, a local restaurant. That summer he worked for a real estate development company, learning critical finacial, analytical, and excel skills.

As he wrapped up college, Jake briefly worked at Resolution Photomatching, did a consulting club, and then did an internship at F5 Networks in a corporate FP&A role.

All together, he has the experience and vision that your company could greatly benefit from.
